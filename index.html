Our Work Environment

The newly renovated space of 70 m2 is fully suitable for children, and includes a fenced yard. The facility is ventilated daily, and cleanliness is maintained exclusively with products based on natural ingredients, without harmful and synthetic substances.

We maintain personal hygiene with natural soaps (homemade or purchased) in order to protect delicate and sensitive children's skin from possible reactions to chemical ingredients.

Our Approach To Childcare

Our concept of keeping and caring for children is based on a holistic approach. Holism understands that man is a unique whole, and through the five holistic aspects of physical, intellectual, emotional, social and spiritual, the mind, body and spirit are connected and one cannot do without the other.

This is how we approach children, our little people, and help develop their skills and their inner empowerment. We listen to the child's interests, potential and talents, and with an individual approach we allow the child to guide us.
