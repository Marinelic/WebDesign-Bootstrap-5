A Little Something About Us

Our small community opened its doors at the beginning of December 2020 for your nursery, kindergarten and preschool children. Dear parents, we provide your children with warmth and security and a feeling of being loved so that they can more easily accept the moment of separation from your embrace, stay with us and return to their homes with bright and happy impressions of our environment. With your child, we will read, create and, through play and various activities, develop his physical, cognitive and emotional development and encourage creative abilities. Our motto is "We grow bite by bite, step by step, word by word, smile by smile!"

A Small Group Of Children. All-day Stay Co-financed By The City Of Zagreb

In a rich, newly renovated space, we offer all-day childcare in a warm family environment, a relaxed and cheerful atmosphere, and learning through play. We focus on safety, hygiene and a healthy and varied diet. Daily guaranteed stay in the fresh air in the yard richly equipped with devices. The proximity of the forest offers us the possibility of walking and learning in nature. We organize trips and other outdoor activities in agreement with parents. As an additional service, we offer night security. We approach each child individually in order to best influence his psychophysical and creative development. All this is provided by an educated, professionally trained babysitter in cooperation with a trained teacher.
